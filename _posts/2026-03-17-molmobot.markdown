MolmoB0T demonstrates that sufficiently large-scale and diverse simulated training data enables effective zero-shot sim-to-real transfer for both static and mobile manipulation, without any real-world fine-tuning. On tabletop pick-and-place, MolmoBot achieves 79.2% real-world success, outperforming π₀.₅ at 39.2%.
