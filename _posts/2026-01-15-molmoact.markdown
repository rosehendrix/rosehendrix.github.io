We introduce Action Reasoning Models (ARMs), a class of robotic foundation models that integrate perception, planning, and control through structured reasoning. MolmoAct encodes observations into depth-aware perception tokens, generates spatial plans as trajectory traces, and predicts precise actions, achieving 70.5% on SimplerEnv and 86.6% on LIBERO with explainable and steerable behavior.
