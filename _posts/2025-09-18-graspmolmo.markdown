GraspMolmo predicts semantically appropriate, stable grasps from natural language instructions and RGB-D frames. Trained on PRISM, a large-scale synthetic dataset of 379k samples, it achieves 70% success on complex real-world tasks compared to 35% for alternatives, with zero-shot bimanual grasping capabilities.
